Transplantation valve to reduce
intraocular pressure - glaucoma - in a specialized hospital


Dr. Ehab Massad, a consultant ophthalmologist, performed a valve implantation to reduce eye pressure - glaucoma at the Ophthalmology Center of the Specialized Hospital for a patient suffering from severe deterioration of the optic nerve that threatens his life with blindness and which was crowned with great success.
