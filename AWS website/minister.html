  2. Dr. Saad F. Jaber

Dr. Saad F. Jaber is a Consultant Cardiovascular Surgeon with over 28 years of experience in the field. He is the Director of Queen Alia Heart Institute which is a branch of the Jordanian Royal Medical Services, providing state of the art Cardiac care for Jordanians and non-Jordanians with open arms all year long. Dr. Saad is also the President of the Jordan Cardiac Society. Dr. Saad Strives not only to provide the most efficient and Evidence-based care but the most professional and the most Up-to-date Care as well. Dr. Saad Leads a team of highly experienced CardiacSurgeons and Cardiologists who have one goal in mind, To Keep Your Heart Well.
